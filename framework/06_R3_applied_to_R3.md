## The Standard for Self-Application

When applying R³ to R³:

1. **Every numerical claim must be independently verifiable.** Provide the calculation. Show the work. Include the code.

2. **Every empirical claim must be labeled.** Distinguish prediction from result. Distinguish calculation from DFT. Distinguish argument from proof.

3. **Every error must be documented.** Not hidden. Not minimized. Documented precisely with the correction.

4. **The correction must be published alongside the original.** Not replacing it. Alongside it. The error and the correction together are more valuable than the correction alone.

5. **No result is final until verified.** Fixed point reached by recursion is a hypothesis. Fixed point confirmed by experiment is a result.
