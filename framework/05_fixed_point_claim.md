## What a Fixed Point Claim Is

A fixed point claim is the public-facing statement of what R³ Composite State Dissolution has found.

It is not a proof. It is a hypothesis.  
It is not a discovery. It is a prediction.  
It is not certain. It is specific.

Specificity is what makes it valuable. A specific prediction can be tested. A tested prediction either advances the field or refines the framework. Both outcomes are progress.
